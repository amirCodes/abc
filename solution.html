INTRUSION DETECTION AND PREVENTION SYSTEMS (IDPS)

iDeras enables you to initiate action by keeping your network safe from intrusion attacks and attempts.

IDPS uses all available information to produce detailed reports to detect any suspicious network activity that could potentially violate security policies. These activities are easily accessible from the management console

With iDeras IDPS, we take things one step further. We don’t just stop at preventing these activities, we actively hunt for insights to determine possible sources, and keep track of them to constantly improve network policies if required

FIREWALL

Guarantees protection for your computers and servers

iDeras recognises that firewalls are an essential part of every network security. That’s why our firewall analyses all data packets to allow communication for trusted applications and parties.

iDeras active firewall provides filters using a range of mediums:
  •   source/destination IP protocol
  •   operating system/network fingerprinting
  •   flexible routing

With modern Layer 7 protocol inspection, we apply the latest technology to your networks with rule based configurable logging.

VPN SOLUTIONS

iDeras meets all the needs of security and user management features required for a Wide Area Network VPN across any location. Designed to scale across hundreds of users, iDeras is the most cost-effective solution for a secure VPN in large organisations.

Multi-location – Deploy appliances anywhere without compromise

Complete range of network security functions – Flexible, Reliable And Interchangeable

WEB FILTERING

Proactively hunts for threats and limits its access by filtering web, document and media file content From web pages to spreadsheets, iDeras takes care of all of it for you.

We filter through a range of documents within the network including, but not limited to: word documents, spreadsheets, webpages and media files.

With iDeras, we do the work so you can be in control. We give you the power to defend your network from potentially dangerous executable programs, including malware by blocking specific types of files from particular users completely.

iDeras allows elaborate policies to be set up while keeping management as simple as possible.

ANTISPAM

Prevent spam and reduce threats related to email messages.

Email is an essential form of digital communication used in every aspect of our modern lives. It is the most common method and the starting point of attacks aimed at organisations.

With IDeras Antispam, we filter all email messages that transit inbound and outbound. We ensure users within the network are protected against infected messages, while preventing these emails from leaving your network.

ACCESS CONTROL

We authenticate and manage users of varied credentials in your network.

iDeras empowers you to access control within all levels of your network. This means that you get to decide permissions levels for users and groups of users for different locations, time-based policies for internet access as well as restrict users to a particular sub-network.

With iDeras, you don’t need a separate user management software.

We work so you can own the decisions.
